The simplest way is to tap "Book Now" on any property card and you'll be connected directly via WhatsApp. You can also browse on Airbnb or drop us a message on Instagram. We confirm bookings within the hour, and you'll receive a full digital guide 24 hours before arrival.
Standard check-in is 2:00 PM and check-out is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are available based on the property's schedule. Just let us know your travel plans on WhatsApp when you book and we'll do our best to accommodate you — usually at no extra cost.
Events and private gatherings are permitted at select properties like Lagoon Farm House and The Backyard Jungle — these are specifically designed for celebrations. Residential properties like The Loft, Casa Woods, Aura Studio, and Mendellin are for private stays only. Always declare your plans at booking so we can make the right recommendation.
We love pets. Select properties are pet-friendly — please mention your furry companion when booking and we'll confirm availability. A small refundable pet deposit may apply to cover any incidentals. We believe in making stays feel like home, and that includes your four-legged family members.
For direct bookings (via WhatsApp), we accept UPI, bank transfer, credit/debit cards, and cash. Airbnb bookings follow Airbnb's payment process. Direct bookings always get the best rate — typically 10–15% lower than Airbnb because there are no platform fees.
Cancellations made 72+ hours before check-in receive a full refund. Cancellations 48–72 hours before check-in receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable. In genuine emergencies, we always try to find a solution — just reach out directly and we'll do our best.
